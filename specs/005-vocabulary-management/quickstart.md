# Quickstart: Vocabulary Management

## Navigation

1. Open the application.
2. Click on the "Vocabulary" (Start/Home) icon in the navigation bar.
3. Access the Tag Manager via the "Tags" tab or a button in the Vocabulary list view.

## Primary Workflows

### Browsing and Filtering

- Use the **Search Bar** at the top to filter by German or Czech terms.
- Click the **Filter Icon** to open the filter drawer/panel.
- Select one or more **Tags** to narrow down the list.
- Use the **Progress Slider/Filter** to see only words in specific Leitner boxes.

### Managing Individual Words

- Click on any word to expand its **Action Menu**.
- Use **"Reset Progress"** to return the word to Box 1.
- Use **"Delete"** to remove the word from your grimoire.
- Add or remove tags directly from the word's card.

### Bulk Actions

- Long-press or click the **Checkbox** on multiple word cards.
- The **Bulk Action Bar** will appear at the bottom.
- Select **"Add Tag"**, **"Remove Tag"**, or **"Delete Selection"**.

### Tag Management

- Navigate to the **Tag Manager**.
- Create new tags for later use.
- Delete tags globally; this will remove them from all assigned words.
