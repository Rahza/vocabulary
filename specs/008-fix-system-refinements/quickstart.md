# Quickstart: Verification

## Shuffling Verification
1. Open the Trainer.
2. Observe the sequence of words.
3. Verify that you don't see the same word in different directions consecutively.

## Mnemonic Verification
1. Go to Vocabulary collection.
2. Open a word detail.
3. Click "Regenerate Mnemonic".
4. The text should update on screen within 1 second.

## Generator Discard Flow
1. Go to Generator.
2. Generate some words.
3. Click "Discard".
4. The confirmation dialog (custom component) should appear.
5. After confirming, the generator form should be visible again (no blank screen).

## Tag Management Navigation
1. Go to Vocabulary collection.
2. Look for the "Manage Tags" or "Tags" tab/link.
3. Verify it navigates to `/tags`.
