# Quickstart: Verification

## Shuffling Verification (Regression Check)

1. Open the Trainer.
2. Verify that no runtime errors occur.

## Stats Verification

1. Open the Dashboard (`/`).
2. Verify that the "Box Verteilung" chart is visible.
3. Verify that the bars in the chart have non-zero heights corresponding to the number of words in each box.

## Practice Page Verification

1. Navigate to the Practice page (`/practice`).
2. Select any tag.
3. Verify that the page loads correctly and does not throw a `ReferenceError`.
